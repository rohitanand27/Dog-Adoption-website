Adoption Process

Learn everything you need to know about adopting your new furry friend!

Adoption Requirements

Before you adopt, ensure you meet these requirements:

  • Be at least 21 years old.
  • Provide proof of residence (lease agreement or utility bill).
  • Consent from all household members for adoption.
  • Adequate space and environment for a dog.

Steps to Adopt

Step 1: Application

Fill out the adoption application with your details and preferences.

Step 2: Meet & Greet

Schedule a visit to meet the dogs and find your perfect match.

Step 3: Home Check

Our team will visit your home to ensure it's safe and suitable for your new pet.

Step 4: Adoption Finalization

Sign the adoption contract and welcome your new furry friend!

Adoption Fees

Your adoption fee helps cover the cost of vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and other medical expenses.

  • Puppies (up to 1 year): 3000
  • Adult Dogs (1-7 years): 2000
  • Senior Dogs (7+ years): 1000
